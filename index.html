Common Concerns

Does it cost money to visit the Guidance Office?
No. Counseling and guidance services are part of your student privileges at LCCM. There are no additional fees for speaking with our professionals.
Will my teachers find out what I talked about?
Your privacy is our priority. All sessions are strictly confidential. Information is only shared if there is an immediate risk to your safety or the safety of others.
Do I need an appointment or can I just walk in?
Walk ins are welcome for urgent concerns. However, to ensure a counselor is available for a full session, we recommend scheduling an appointment via email or visiting the office in person.
